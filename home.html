    Help

    Search for recipes or try "top 5" for the top voted dishes in your area
    Navigate through the map by dragging or selecting markers to choose local recipes
    View recipes in the window on the right. You can reopen it by clicking on the "view recipe" tab, if you choose to close it.
    With an account, you can upvote recipes and add your own. When you go to your profile, you can edit your dietary restrictions, see all your own recipes, and delete any of your recipes.
    "I'm feeling lucky", under features, shows you a recipe made with cauliflower
    The heatmap, under features, lets you compare which recipes have the most views or recipes.
    Compass helps you find your direction - travel is so broad. You can go almost anywhere! Get some travel inspiration and see where your friends and people like you have gone. You can search around the map to find places that interest you, and read TripAdvisor reviews by people you can relate to.
